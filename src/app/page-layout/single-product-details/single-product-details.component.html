Lit Channel Letters Logos

Commonly called "Channel Letters" or “Face Lit Letters” The way the signs are mounted and how they are lit determines their further description. For instance, if the sign is lit through the front it is called Face Lit or BackLit. If the sign lights out the back it is called Halo Lit or Reverse Lit.

This can get confusing so we have illustrated the sign types here so you can choose a product to continue learning about.
